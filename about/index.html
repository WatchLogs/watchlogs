Hello. I am a writer and web developer based in San Francisco. This site is a recent effort to consolidate all of the writing I've published in disparate places over the past five years. It covers everything from backpacking in South America (2012) to reporting in Brooklyn (2011) all the way to more recent writings on design, data, algorithms, and Stoicism (2015-16).

These days I think a lot about the relationship between technology and humanity. Specifically, how does technology, something we as humans create, come back to affect us? As AI becomes a more prominent fixture in our everyday lives, what will our future look like? And most importantly, in what ways can we use technology to improve ourselves and the lives of others?

When I'm not at a computer, I like to run, cook, and ride my bike. Sometimes I draw, paint, and play the piano. I geek out over architecture, urban planning, and fitness. I try to meditate every day. I listen to way too many podcasts.

This is what I look like.
